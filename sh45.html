Managing people: your thoughts


2333 command


Are you are an aspiring manager? Do you already manage your own team or lead a project group? Whatever your experience in managing people this course will offer you some interesting and useful insights and skills.


Over the next five weeks you’ll be learning alongside a diverse community of people from around the world with a huge range of experience and knowledge. Take a moment to introduce yourself to your fellow learners and share a little about why you’ve joined this course.


Your task


Choose one (or both) of the prompts below and share your thoughts.


1.What do you believe is the most interesting part of managing people?


2.What do you think is the most challenging aspect of managing people?


Here are some ideas to get you started:


-Do you enjoy the opportunity to develop an individual or a team?


-Do you like motivating a team to complete a particular task or project?


-Do you find the prospect of performance management daunting?


-Are you unsure how to integrate the organisations well-being initiatives into your team?


Whatever your thoughts, we’d like to hear them. Add your thoughts to the discussion below and see what other learners are sharing.

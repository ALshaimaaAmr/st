Welcome to Introduction to Intercultural Studies

Your lead educator for this course is Dr Haynes Collins, a lecturer in Intercultural Studies at the University of Leeds.

This course introduces the main ideas of culture and interculturality. You will develop your understanding of the range of meanings of the term ‘culture’ and explore the effects of the interaction between cultures. You will also develop your understanding of interculturality – the interaction of people from different cultural backgrounds in a way that demonstrates an understanding of the other cultures. In addition, you will examine the theories that underpin the study of these interactions.

Learning for General Interest

If you have a general interest in this topic, you can work through just the core learning activities – this should take around four hours each week.

You will have the opportunity to check your understanding and spend some time joining the discussions. It is important that you don’t feel pressured to complete all the elements of the course. Your learning journey is individual, and you should decide what elements are relevant to your learning needs.

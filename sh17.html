Learning on this Course

Throughout this course, you will have the opportunity to meet other learners, share your ideas, and engage in active discussions in the comments.

What Will You Achieve?

  • Identify the myths and symbols of the olive tree.
  • Describe the origins and development of olive cultivation.
  • Explore the production of olive oil.
  • Identify the French production regions, their actors, and their specificities.
  • Describe the emblematic varieties of olive oil in the market.
  • Explore the marketing criteria for olive oil.
  • Apply the benefits of extra virgin olive oil in cooking.
  • Identify the key principles of olive oil tasting.

Who is the Course For?

Whether you're a cooking enthusiast, a culinary professional, or simply curious, this course offers a unique opportunity to discover the benefits of olive oil and the Mediterranean diet. Join us for a sensory and flavorful adventure that will transform your approach to cooking and health!

Fundraising from everyman (
and woman)


99 comments


There are many ways to ask individual people to give your cause,
group or charity a donation and there are also different ways to then
help them to give it to you, some of which we will explore in more
detail in this step.


In this step we will consider the ways in which the “everyman” (Hansen, 2017) donor can be encouraged to give to your organisation. In the next
step, we will be joined by Beth Breeze, who will explore how donors
choose which charities and good causes they will give to.


Which methods you choose to use will be based on an evaluation of what
you think interests your potential supporter, how much you
think they are able to give, and what will be motivating them to give - as well as the
time and resources each method is likely to require.


References

Hansen, R. (2017) Do Fundraisers Write Different Appeals for Stigmatized Groups? Paper presentation at: 46th International Association for Research in Nonprofit Organizations and Voluntary Action Conference, 16-18 November 2017, Grand Rapids, MI, USA.
